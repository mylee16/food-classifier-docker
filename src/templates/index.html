Model Specifications

This food classifier was built using Imagenet pretrained MobileNetv2 architecture layered with a 128 node dense layer and a 12 node dense layers for classification. The model was trained on 800 images of 12 different classes of Singaporean food on 10 epochs, with early stopping when validation loss with a patience of 2. The Adam optimizer was used with a learning rate of 1e-3. The model was compiled using categorical cross-entropy loss.

The model achieved accuracy of 85% with an estimated training time of 30 minutes.
